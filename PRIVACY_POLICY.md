# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## English

ShellPlayer is a local music player application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

### Data Collection
ShellPlayer does **NOT** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device.

### What the App Accesses
- **Music Files**: ShellPlayer scans directories on your device to find and play audio files. All files remain on your device.
- **Media Library (iOS)**: With your permission, ShellPlayer can import music from your iTunes/iPod library. This data never leaves your device.
- **Microphone (Android)**: The audio visualization feature uses the Android Visualizer API, which requires the RECORD_AUDIO permission. ShellPlayer does NOT record or store any audio — this permission is only used for real-time audio level metering.

### Data Storage
All app data (favorites, playlists, play history, settings) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. No data is transmitted to any server.

### Third-Party Services
ShellPlayer does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

### In-App Purchases
ShellPlayer Pro is available as a one-time purchase through the App Store / Google Play. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple / Google. We do not have access to your payment information.

### Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: shellplayer@example.com

## 中文

ShellPlayer 是一款本地音乐播放器应用。我们致力于保护您的隐私。

### 数据收集
ShellPlayer **不会**收集、存储、传输或分享任何个人数据。应用完全在您的设备上运行。

### 应用访问的内容
- **音乐文件**：ShellPlayer 扫描您设备上的目录以查找和播放音频文件。所有文件保留在您的设备上。
- **媒体库（iOS）**：经您许可，ShellPlayer 可以从 iTunes/iPod 音乐库导入音乐。这些数据不会离开您的设备。
- **麦克风（Android）**：音频可视化功能使用 Android Visualizer API，需要 RECORD_AUDIO 权限。ShellPlayer 不会录制或存储任何音频 — 此权限仅用于实时音频电平检测。

### 数据存储
所有应用数据（收藏、歌单、播放历史、设置）均使用 AsyncStorage 存储在您的设备本地。没有数据会传输到任何服务器。

### 第三方服务
ShellPlayer 不集成任何分析、广告或追踪服务。

### 应用内购买
ShellPlayer Pro 可通过 App Store / Google Play 一次性购买。购买交易完全由 Apple / Google 处理。我们无法访问您的支付信息。

### 联系方式
如对本隐私政策有疑问，请联系：shellplayer@example.com
